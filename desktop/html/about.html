About Me

I'm a trans girl in her 30's that has a lot to say. I've got an opinion about fuckin' everything. I'm probably one of the most hot-headed, stubborn dumbasses you'll ever meet! I play bass and guitar and never know when to turn down. I believe in personal freedom and agency. The freedom to define, redefine, and reframe yourself is very important to me. I'm a privacy advocate. I just generally like getting into stupid shit. I've been teaching myself HTML, CSS, JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, and C since early 2024. I want to learn more about computers and what makes them tick, and how to work with them. I want to see more of the world. And I will. I'm a math geek but I like to get lost in my own little worlds too.

That's enough for now. You can look around for more.
